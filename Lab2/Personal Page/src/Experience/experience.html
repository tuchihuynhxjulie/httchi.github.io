INTERACTIVE CHART APP

Technologies: Javascript, ReactJs, Apexchart, antd, Firebase, DSA

An interactive chart app is a piece of software that enablesusers to dynamically and engrossingly create, alter, and interact with charts and graphs. Users can enter data and have it converted into a variety of graphic representations, including pie charts, scatter plots, bar charts, line graphs, and more. An interactive chart app's capacity to allow users to dynamically change and examine the chart is its defining feature. In order to study the data from numerous perspectives, users frequently interact with the data points, pan across the chart, zoom in or out, and apply various filters. They might also have the choice to click on a specific area of a pie chart to dig down into subcategories, hover over data points to view more granular information or do any number of other things.

CHEST X-RAY IMAGES

Technologies: Modes in Machine Learning & AI

We use some model in Machine Learning to train dataset.The dataset consists of 5,863 chest X-ray images (JPEG) categorized into Pneumonia and Normal, organized into three folders (train, test, and validation) with subfolders for each category. The images were taken from pediatric patients aged one to five at Guangzhou Women and Children’s Medical Center. All X-rays were screened for quality, and the diagnoses were graded by two expert physicians. A third expert reviewed the evaluation set to ensure accuracy, aiming to reduce grading errors before training an AI system.

Link Github: https://github.com/tuchihuynhxjulie/Chest-X-ray-Images
