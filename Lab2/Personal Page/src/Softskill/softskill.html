WELCOME FRESHMEN

Category: Welcome new students

Event organizational team: IT Youth Union

Description: "Welcome Freshmen" is an annual event organized by departments to welcome new students and familiarize them with their respective faculties. The program features introductions to faculty members, club presentations, and recognition of high-achieving students who received scholarships in the recent national high school examination. Its primary goal is to provide freshmen with a deeper understanding of their academic environment, encourage involvement in extracurricular activities, and celebrate academic achievements.

My role: Organizing committee - Prepare all gifts for more than 200 new students and plan to rent a fully equipped location to organize the program.

IT HACKATHON

Category: Academic

Event organizational team: IT Youth Union

Description: The IT Hackathon is an academic program organized by the ITYU, where participants form teams to brainstorm ideas and develop products within a 24-hour timeframe under the guidance of experienced mentors. Following intense collaboration and coding sessions, teams present their ideas to a panel of judges for evaluation and ranking. This event fosters creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills among participants while providing a platform to showcase innovative solutions to real-world challenges in the field of technology.

My role: Organizing committee - Come up with ideas for decorating the exam rooms and prepare logistics for 200 people including judges, candidates, and other attendees.

SPRING VOLUNTEER

Category: Charitable

Event organizational team: Student Association & IT Youth Union

Description: The Spring Volunteer program is an annual event organized during the spring season by the student union in collaboration with various departmental organizations. It involves visiting local communities to engage in activities such as cleaning streets and distributing gifts to residents. Prior to the community visits, there are cultural activities, including making traditional rice cakes, known as "bánh chưng," accompanied by festive performances to celebrate the Lunar New Year. This program aims to promote community involvement, cultural exchange, and the spirit of volunteerism among students while fostering goodwill and contributing to the betterment of local communities.

My role: Organizing committee - Come up with ideas to redecorate the school yard, organize entertainment activities for the children along with fully preparing gifts to go to each poor household to give gifts to the people.

LORD OF PUZZLE

Category: Culture - History

Event organizational team: Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union of International University

Description: "Lord of Puzzle" is a cultural and historical knowledge contest organized by the International University Youth Union. The competition pits representatives from various faculties against each other, challenging their knowledge of cultural heritage and history. Questions can range from multiple-choice to essay formats, focusing on significant moments and achievements in Vietnamese cultural history. The competition celebrates the rich cultural heritage of Vietnam and honors the victorious spirit of its people.

My role: Attendee - Answer questions about historical relics and national heroes by drawing from their academic studies and life experiences. This competition encourages participants to showcase their knowledge of Vietnamese history and culture in a concise and impactful manner.
